Hey Trainer!

Welcome to the Maryland Pokemon Go Community! We have a thriving community of players across the entire state who join together to reach their pokemon dreams! Don't forget to check out our Events page to check out current and upcoming events!


Gotta Catch Them All

We are a tight knit community of Pokemon Go players all throughout the great state of Maryland! Our members are all super welcoming, supportive, and we've got a large player base of all ages. We regularly host different meetups to take advantage of in-game events like Spotlight Hours, Community Days, and more!

We have some awesome local vendors selling a variety of Pokemon themed merchandise. Our talented friends can set you up with some awesome stuffed plushes, jewelry, custom bags, apparel, and even some tasty treats!

Most of our events also have free to enter raffles for cool prizes like pins, magnets, and other swag. Everyone is welcome to show up, hang out, and maybe end the day with some new friends.
